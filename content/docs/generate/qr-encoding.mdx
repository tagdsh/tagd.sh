## QR recommendations

- Encode only the Signal Tag ID or a short resolver URL.
- Keep payload short for faster scan reliability.
- Use ECC level suitable for packaging conditions.

## Example strategy

- `st_01HZXK7M2N3P4Q5R6S7T8U9V`
- or `https://verify.example.com/st/st_01HZXK7M2N3P4Q5R6S7T8U9V`
